NovaRetail Risk & Strategic Recommendation Guidelines

1. Profitability Risk:
If profit margin falls below 20%, this indicates financial risk.
Recommended actions:
- Review pricing strategy
- Reduce supplier costs
- Optimize operational expenses
- Discontinue low-margin products

2. Revenue Growth Risk:
If revenue growth is below 5% or negative:
- Increase marketing investment
- Launch promotional campaigns
- Evaluate product competitiveness
- Explore new customer segments

3. Regional Underperformance:
If a region contributes less than 20% of total revenue:
- Conduct regional market analysis
- Adjust local pricing strategy
- Improve store operations
- Increase regional marketing efforts

4. Product Risk:
If a product margin is below 15%:
- Reevaluate cost structure
- Consider bundling strategy
- Negotiate supplier contracts
- Consider discontinuation

5. Channel Risk:
If online sales contribute less than 30% of total revenue:
- Improve digital marketing
- Enhance website performance
- Launch online-exclusive offers

All recommendations should align with long-term sustainable growth and profitability objectives.
