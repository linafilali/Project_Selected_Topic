NovaRetail Industry Benchmark Summary – Retail Electronics Sector

1. Industry Profit Margin:
- Average gross margin in retail electronics ranges between 22% and 28%.
- High-performing companies maintain margins above 30%.

2. Industry Revenue Growth:
- Typical annual revenue growth in stable markets ranges between 8% and 12%.
- Growth below 5% is considered weak.
- Negative growth signals market or competitive challenges.

3. Online Channel Contribution:
- Industry standard for online sales contribution is between 35% and 45% of total revenue.
- Companies below 30% online contribution are considered digitally underdeveloped.

4. Cost Structure:
- Typical cost-to-revenue ratio ranges between 70% and 75%.
- Ratios above 80% indicate operational inefficiencies.

5. Regional Diversification:
- Balanced regional performance is preferred.
- Overdependence on a single region (>50%) increases strategic risk.

All benchmark comparisons should consider long-term competitiveness and sustainability.
