NovaRetail KPI Threshold & Interpretation Rules

1. Profit Margin Interpretation:
- Above 30% → Strong Performance
- Between 25% and 30% → Healthy Performance
- Between 20% and 25% → Warning Level
- Below 20% → High Financial Risk

2. Revenue Growth Rate:
- Above 12% → Excellent Growth
- Between 8% and 12% → Healthy Growth
- Between 5% and 8% → Moderate Growth
- Below 5% → Growth Concern
- Negative Growth → Critical Performance Issue

3. Cost-to-Revenue Ratio:
- Below 70% → Efficient Cost Structure
- Between 70% and 80% → Acceptable
- Above 80% → Cost Control Risk

4. Regional Contribution:
- Above 35% of total revenue → Core Region
- Between 20% and 35% → Stable Region
- Below 20% → Underperforming Region

5. Product Performance:
- Products contributing to top 20% of revenue → Strategic Products
- Products with margin below 15% → Low Margin Risk

All KPI interpretations should align with sustainable profitability and long-term growth objectives.
